Ol' Boozy

In Port Richmond, we don't beat around the bush, and neither does Ol' Boozy, a former sailing bird found abandoned on pier 18 (Graffiti Pier). His mates must have had a swift exit and forgotten about Ol' Boozy. He's a bird that knows a thing or two about ocean currents and the best rum pairings for hard tack and grease grits. Take him home today, and begin a sea farin' life tomorrow with your competent first mate. Yargh.
